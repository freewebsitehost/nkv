Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and financial planning

What makes NKV Financial Navigator different from other financial advisors?

We take a holistic approach to financial planning, considering all aspects of your financial life rather than focusing on isolated products or services. Our team of certified professionals uses cutting-edge technology combined with personalized service to create strategies that evolve with your changing needs.

How are your fees structured?

We operate on a fee-only basis, which means we don't earn commissions from product sales. Our fees are transparent and based on the complexity of your financial situation and the services you require. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a clear explanation of all costs before you make any commitments.

What is your investment philosophy?

We believe in evidence-based investing that focuses on long-term strategies rather than short-term market timing. Our approach emphasizes diversification, cost efficiency, and aligning investments with your specific risk tolerance and financial goals. We regularly review and rebalance portfolios to maintain target allocations.

How often will we meet to review my financial plan?

We typically schedule quarterly reviews for active clients, but the frequency can be adjusted based on your needs. Between formal meetings, we're always available to address questions or concerns that may arise. Major life events like marriage, career changes, or inheritance will prompt additional reviews.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, we serve clients nationwide through secure video conferencing and digital collaboration tools. Our technology platform allows us to provide the same high level of service to remote clients as we do to those who visit our office in person.

What credentials do your advisors hold?

Our team includes Certified Financial Planners (CFPÂ®), Chartered Financial Analysts (CFA), and other industry-certified professionals. We maintain these credentials through ongoing education and adhere to strict ethical standards. You can verify our credentials through the CFP Board and CFA Institute websites.
